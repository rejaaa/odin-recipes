World' Best Egg Benedict

How to make the world's best Egg Benedict?

Eggs Benedict, it's the brunch classic that always impresses, and it's easier to make than you might think. We'll show you how to do it.

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Pan
  • whatever else you like; bread, cheese, etc.

How to make them?

  1. Poach the egg. Add a tablespoon of vinegar to a pan of water and bring it just to a gentel simmer. Crack the egg first into a separate container. Now stir the water in a wide circle and add the whole egg.
  2. Make the hollandaise sauce. Hollandaise sauce is an emulsion of eggs and butter. Blend the eggs and butter.
  3. Serve the egg top with the hollandaise sauce.
